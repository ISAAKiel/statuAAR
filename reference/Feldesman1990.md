# Feldesman1990: statuaar table example

A measurements table from *Feldesman et al 1990, Tab. 6*. Raw data,
femur/stature estimates, and Trotter-Gleser stature estimates for sample
of mid-to late-pleistocene fossil hominids. (1) Gender according to
published criteria: (a) pubic morphology or (b) cranial capacity and
skeletal.

## Format

A data frame with 48 rows and 7 variables.

- **site** site name abreviation

- **Type** hominide

- **Probable.gender:** M(a/b), F(a/b) -\> Male, Female

- **Gender.reference** reference for gender

- **Femur.length** measures (mm)

- **Femur.stature** stature calculation based on Femur (mm)

- **Trotter-Gleser.stature** stature calculation based Trotter & Gleser
  (mm)

## References

Feldesman MR, Kleckner JG, Lundy JK (1990). “Femur/stature ratio and
estimates of stature in mid- and late-pleistocene fossil hominids.”
*American Journal of Physical Anthropology*, **83**(3), 359–372. ISSN
1096-8644,
[doi:10.1002/ajpa.1330830309](https://doi.org/10.1002/ajpa.1330830309) .
