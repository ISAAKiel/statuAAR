# dl.trotter.gleser: statuaar data table

A measures data table (list form) from *Trotter & Gleser 1952, Appendix
1-4*.

## Format

A data frame with 100 rows and 18 variables.

- **Ind** identifier

- **Sex:** f, m, i -\> female, male, indet.

- **Variable** bone measure

- **value** measures mm

## References

Trotter M, Gleser GC (1952). “Estimation of stature from long bones of
American Whites and Negroes.” *American Journal of Physical
Anthropology*, **10**(4), 463–514. ISSN 1096-8644,
[doi:10.1002/ajpa.1330100407](https://doi.org/10.1002/ajpa.1330100407) .
