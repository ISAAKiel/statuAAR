# Rollet1888: statuaar table example

A measurements table from *Rollet 1888, pp. 16-23*. Data as given
without calculated columns and stature in millimeters as for the
measures. Further explanation mentions a general shrinking of the bones
by app. 2 mm within eight to ten months after the first measures in
fresh condition. This differentiation is shown in the tables for the
femora only. (Rollet 1888 p.24)

## Format

A data frame with 100 rows and 18 variables.

- **Nr** identifier

- **Sex:** f, m -\> female, male

- **Age** in years

- **Stature** measures mm

- **Femur.right** measures mm

- **Femur.left** measures mm

- **Femur.right.dry** measures mm

- **Femur.left.dry** measures mm

- **Tibia.right** measures mm

- **Tibia.left** measures mm

- **Fibula.right** measures mm

- **Fibula.left** measures mm

- **Humerus.right** measures mm

- **Humerus.left** measures mm

- **Radius.right** measures mm

- **Radius.left** measures mm

- **Ulna.right** measures mm

- **Ulna.left** measures mm
